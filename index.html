WallaHop – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2025

Overview

WallaHop is a macOS app that lets you save web pages to your Wallabag account via the Share menu. This policy describes how WallaHop handles your data.

Data Storage

WallaHop stores the following information only on your Mac, in a secure app container:

  • Your Wallabag API URL
  • Your API Client ID and Client Secret
  • Your Wallabag username and password

This data is used solely to authenticate with your Wallabag instance. It is never sent to any server other than your own Wallabag server (e.g. app.wallabag.it or your self-hosted instance).

Data Collection

WallaHop does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising.

Network Usage

WallaHop connects to the Wallabag server you configure. All communication uses HTTPS. URLs you save are sent only to your Wallabag instance.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer through your preferred App Store feedback channel.
